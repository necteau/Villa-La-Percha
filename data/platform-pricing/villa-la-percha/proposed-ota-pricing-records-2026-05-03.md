- Airbnb/VRBO did not expose usable Dec 24–31 or Dec 26–Jan 2 New Year quotes in this pass, so I did not invent a New Year record. If you want New Year represented anyway, use the Christmas holiday band as a placeholder pending manual/owner confirmation.
- January–April peak months are not included because the requested endpoint was 12/31/26 and those months have already passed for 2026. If we want forward-looking seasonal templates, the next pass should query Jan–Apr 2027.

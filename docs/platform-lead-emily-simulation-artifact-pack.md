## Preview Build Rationale Draft

A Preview Build is useful now because Emily needs to see how a DirectStay-owned presentation could feel without committing to launch or changing existing operations. The preview should be public-obscure/noindex, clearly non-functional, and structured as an owner review artifact.

**Required callouts**

- Photo rights and preferred gallery order need owner confirmation.
- Bedroom/bath/occupancy, lake access, parking, dock rules, and HOA constraints are assumptions until Emily confirms.
- Pricing, fees, taxes, minimum stays, cancellation, and payment flow are not live.
- Calendar, inquiry, booking, and payment actions must be disabled or clearly marked preview-only.

**Design direction**

- Calm lake-house positioning, not generic luxury.
- Emphasize screened porch, private dock, repeat-family stays, and low-friction direct return bookings.
- Use owner-truth language: “what guests come back for,” “how the house actually lives,” and “clear rules before launch.”

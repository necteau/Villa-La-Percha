# Villa La Percha — Phase 2 Booking Plan

## Goal
Turn the site from a polished brochure into a booking engine that consistently generates qualified direct inquiries and converts them into paid stays.

## Primary Strategy
Focus first on channels that capture **high-intent demand** or **high-trust referrals** before spending energy on broad awareness campaigns.

## Recommended Channel Priority

### Tier 1 — Start Here
1. **Existing OTA listing traffic -> direct-booking comparison story**
   - Use Airbnb/VRBO as demand proof and pricing benchmarks.
   - Populate the new pricing table with actual OTA totals vs direct totals.
   - Use that comparison on-site to make the savings claim concrete.

2. **Google Search / SEO**
   - Best long-term channel for direct-booking intent.
   - Prioritize searches around:
     - Chalk Sound villa rental
     - Taylor Bay villa rental
     - Sapodilla Bay villa rental
     - Turks and Caicos family villa
     - Providenciales waterfront villa
   - Build trust pages and FAQ content before paid acquisition.

3. **Warm network / referrals / past guests**
   - Highest trust, lowest friction, best early bookings.
   - Create shareable copy and a simple referral message set.

4. **Instagram / Facebook organic + paid later**
   - Strong fit because the property is visual and the location story is distinctive.
   - Use mostly for credibility, remarketing, and top-of-funnel inspiration — not as the only conversion channel.

### Tier 2 — Add After Tier 1 Is Ready
5. **Google Ads / branded + high-intent search campaigns**
   - Only after analytics and conversion tracking exist.
   - Focus on high-intent keywords, not broad luxury-travel vanity traffic.

6. **Luxury villa directories / local partnerships**
   - Possible fit if the CAC is reasonable and listing quality is high.
   - Evaluate manually before account creation.

7. **Email capture / nurture**
   - Useful once there is repeat traffic or a past-guest list.
   - Not urgent before traffic and inquiry flow are established.

## What Must Exist Before Paid Marketing
- Live inquiry delivery via Resend
- Analytics and event tracking plan in place
- Exact price comparison entries for at least several live date ranges
- Strong trust signals:
  - accurate property facts
  - polished gallery
  - direct-booking explanation
  - location clarity
  - FAQ
  - response SLA (within 24 hours)

## Conversion Priorities
1. Make direct savings concrete with exact date-based comparisons
2. Show trust and legitimacy
3. Reduce friction in the inquiry flow
4. Respond to inquiries quickly and consistently

## Messaging Pillars
1. **Better total value**
   - Direct bookings are typically 15–30% lower than Airbnb/VRBO total pricing.
2. **Better location story**
   - Chalk Sound neighborhood between Taylor Bay and Sapodilla Bay.
3. **Better stay experience**
   - Pool, hot tub, dock swimming, fishing, paddling, indoor-outdoor living.
4. **Better fit for groups**
   - 4 en-suite suites + common spaces that actually make group travel easy.

## Metrics to Track
- Inquiry submissions
- Inquiry-to-response time
- Inquiry-to-booking rate
- Bookings by source
- Revenue by source
- Average stay length
- Date ranges with strongest conversion
- OTA vs direct savings demonstrated on-site

## 30-Day Recommended Execution Order
1. Finish Resend setup and live inquiry test
2. Populate pricing table with real direct / Airbnb / VRBO date ranges
3. Add FAQ page or FAQ section
4. Set up analytics plan and event definitions
5. Create referral/share copy
6. Prepare organic social asset set
7. Evaluate Google Search / Meta only after the above are live

## Decisions Already Made
- No co-ownership/shared-use language on guest-facing pages
- Direct-booking positioning should emphasize total-cost savings vs OTAs
- Fishing is a real differentiator and should be supported with real photos when available

## Not Recommended Yet
- Creating a pile of external accounts before the funnel is instrumented
- Broad paid social for cold traffic before trust and conversion proof are stronger
- Expanding to too many directories before tracking source quality

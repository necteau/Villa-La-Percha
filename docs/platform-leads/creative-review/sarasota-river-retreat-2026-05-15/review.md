## Process Finding

The combined creative-review loop worked. The three variants forced real design divergence instead of minor palette swaps, and the scoring made the tradeoff obvious: the most creative version was not the safest production base, while the most useful version needed warmth borrowed from the others.

Next process improvement: always require the final selected Preview Build to include a short "borrowed from losing variants" section so the winner does not become visually bland.

# DirectStay Owner Acquisition Engine — 2026-05-09

Purpose: create a repeatable, approval-gated owner-acquisition workflow for DirectStay PlatformLeads. This is internal operating material only. No owner contact has been sent.

## Operating boundary

- Target segment: individual short-term-rental owners with one strong property or a small personal portfolio.
- Initial wedge: show what DirectStay can do with a property-specific Preview Build, not generic “we can build websites” pitch copy.
- External emails, DMs, calls, proposals, owner-share preview links, and contract/signature activation all require Jaimal approval.
- Preview Builds remain non-functional sales/onboarding artifacts until contract and launch gates are cleared.

## Qualification rubric

Score 0–3 in each category; prioritize leads scoring 13+ out of 18.

| Dimension | 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Property distinctiveness | generic condo | competent but common | strong amenity/location hook | unforgettable stay narrative |
| Direct-booking fit | no clear benefit | possible fee savings | clear repeat/referral potential | obvious OTA dependency + premium direct story |
| Source quality | unreadable/sparse | title/snippets only | readable listing/site copy | owner/property site + photos + OTA proof |
| Owner plausibility | aggregator/brand only | unclear owner | small operator likely | owner site/brand visible |
| Preview-build leverage | hard to differentiate | moderate | strong modules possible | design/facts/calendar/price guide can shine |
| Risk/complexity | rights/legal high | many unknowns | manageable assumptions | clean facts, clear next questions |

## AI-led workflow

1. **Source discovery**
   - Search by destination/property archetype.
   - Prefer owner/property websites, then OTA listings with enough readable details.
   - Capture source URLs and observed facts; do not infer facts from vibes.

2. **Lead brief**
   - Property thesis, owner fit, source evidence, missing confirmations, likely direct-booking value, and a recommended first ask.

3. **Preview decision**
   - Hot lead + enough source truth → internal Preview Build candidate.
   - Thin source truth → research-only lead until image/fact gate clears.

4. **Approval pack**
   - Present Jaimal with: source, why this lead, first-contact draft, and whether a preview should be built before outreach.

5. **First outreach after approval only**
   - Short, property-specific, non-pushy discovery note.
   - No pricing claims, no guaranteed savings, no implied partnership until owner engages.

6. **Admin/Mission Control memory**
   - Record status, source links, brief, approval state, sent/unsent drafts, preview artifact path, and blockers.

# Overview

Historic/classic properties need respect, texture, and restraint. The preview should elevate character without making the property feel dusty.

## Colors

Extract from original materials, wallpaper, trim, stone, brick, garden, antiques, artwork, and metal details. Use accent sparingly.

## Typography

Serif-led or heritage-informed type works well, but avoid parody. Modernize through spacing and composition rather than stripping character away.

## Layout

- Editorial story sections.
- Detail photography: doors, fireplaces, gardens, stairs, porch, original features.
- Practical confidence should coexist with romance.

## Do's and Don'ts

Do: preserve character and specificity.
Don't: flatten into modern SaaS minimalism or fake old-world luxury.

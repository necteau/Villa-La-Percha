# Overview

Modern design stays should feel intentional and art-directed. Use fewer, stronger modules; large crops; material closeups; asymmetric editorial rhythm.

## Colors

Extract from architecture and interiors: plaster, concrete, oak, marble, black metal, upholstery, art objects. Avoid arbitrary travel colors.

## Typography

Crisp and confident. More tension, less softness. Use restraint.

## Layout

- Strong grid and asymmetry.
- Big image crops.
- Minimal but specific copy.
- Amenities integrated as design/practical details, not icon soup.

## Do's and Don'ts

Do: let architecture and material choices lead.
Don't: make it a generic black/white tech landing page.

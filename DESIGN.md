# DESIGN.md

## Product personality

Villa La Percha should feel like a highly curated private-island travel advisor: warm, precise, editorial, quietly luxurious, and specific to Chalk Sound/Taylor Bay rather than generic “luxury villa” copy.

## Audience / use context

Affluent families, couples traveling together, and group-trip planners researching Turks and Caicos villas. They are comparing resorts, Airbnb/VRBO listings, and private villa sites. They need confidence, not hype.

## Inspiration references

- High-end boutique travel editorial: vivid place writing, practical insider detail, restrained calls to action.
- Luxury villa brochures: large imagery, calm spacing, feature-led planning blocks.
- Modern travel guides: scannable local context, “best for” guidance, clear next steps.

## Visual direction

- Mood: sun-warmed, salt-air, specific, uncluttered.
- Layout: cinematic hero, editorial article body, alternating image/story sections, practical planning cards.
- Color: existing DirectStay/Villa palette — deep green, sand, warm white, muted gold.
- Typography: elegant display headings with readable body copy.
- Components: image-led sections, pull quotes, “best for” / “what to know” cards, links into FAQ/island guide/availability.
- Motion/interactions: keep subtle; SEO pages should be fast and calm.

## Anti-patterns to avoid

- Repeating the same amenity list on every page.
- Thin SEO doorway pages.
- Generic “paradise awaits” travel copy.
- Overpromising exact savings, amenities, or conditions beyond booking-confirmed facts.
- Dense walls of text without photos or planning utility.

## Mobile behavior

Single-column editorial flow, images above/near related copy, sticky-feeling clarity through short sections and compact planning cards.

## Acceptance checklist

- Each guide has a distinct angle and vocabulary.
- Each page uses a different lead image and supporting image.
- Each page answers real search intent with practical detail.
- Internal links connect to Villa page, FAQ, island guide, itinerary, and availability.
- Structured data remains valid and build passes.

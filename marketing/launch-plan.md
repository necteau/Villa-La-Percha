# Villa La Percha Launch Marketing Plan

Status: site approved for launch/go-live by Jaimal on 2026-05-07.

## Goal
Drive qualified traffic to the Villa La Percha direct-booking site and convert it into direct inquiries/bookings.

Primary URL: https://directstay.app/villa-la-percha

## Core positioning
- Private Chalk Sound villa near Taylor Bay and Sapodilla Bay.
- Book direct to avoid unnecessary platform markup and get direct communication.
- Best fit: families, couples traveling together, and small groups who want privacy, pool/hot tub, dock access, outdoor living, and easy beach access.
- Credibility: approved guest reviews, real villa photos, detailed FAQ, island guide, and direct owner/operator response.

## Guardrails
- External posts, DMs, emails, partnership outreach, paid ads, and spend require Jaimal approval before execution.
- Do not imply Airbnb/VRBO endorsement.
- Do not use fake/uncleared testimonials.
- Track every outreach/action with source, status, date, evidence, and result.

## Workstreams
1. Network announcement — personal/Emily/family/friend launch copy and light sharing.
2. Travel planner / concierge outreach — TCI planners, villa concierges, boutique agencies, family-trip/wedding planners.
3. Local/destination groups — Facebook, Reddit, and travel communities where helpful participation is allowed.
4. SEO/content distribution — share/strengthen guide pages and direct-booking education.
5. Direct-booking assets — one-sheet, captions, mini pitch, reusable blurbs.
6. Performance tracking — traffic baseline, source attribution, inquiry quality, booking conversion.

## First approval pack to prepare
- Personal/network launch announcement.
- Emily-friendly version.
- Travel planner outreach email.
- Concierge/partner DM.
- TCI group post/comment draft.
- Short direct-booking explainer.

## Source attribution checklist
For each inquiry, capture where possible:
- Referrer/source/channel.
- UTM parameters if present.
- Guest self-reported source.
- Outreach item or post that may have driven it.
- Inquiry quality: dates, group size, budget/fit, booking likelihood.
- Outcome: replied, serious lead, booked, lost, follow-up needed.

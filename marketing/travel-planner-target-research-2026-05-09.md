## Better first-pass target strategy

1. **Warm-network travel advisors** — safest and highest trust if Jaimal knows anyone directly or through family/friends.
2. **Independent luxury travel advisors who plan Caribbean family trips** — less directly competitive than local villa managers.
3. **Local concierge/service providers who complement stays** — chefs, boat captains, grocery provisioning, photographers; could share guide value without competing on lodging.
4. **Marketplaces/agencies** — evaluate only if Jaimal wants non-OTA distribution; terms may conflict with direct-booking goals.

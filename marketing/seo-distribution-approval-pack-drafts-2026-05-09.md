## Candidate B — helpful destination-group post

**Draft**

A quick planning note for anyone comparing Chalk Sound/Taylor Bay vs. Grace Bay:

Grace Bay is best if you want resort density, walkable restaurants, and the classic beach-strip experience. Chalk Sound is better if your trip is more private-villa focused: quieter nights, dramatic water views, Taylor Bay/Sapodilla Bay nearby, and more of a “stock the fridge and settle in” rhythm.

I put together a Chalk Sound villa planning guide here if it helps anyone compare the areas:
https://directstay.app/villa-la-percha/guides/chalk-sound-villa-rental

Happy to answer location questions — the two areas really fit different trip styles.

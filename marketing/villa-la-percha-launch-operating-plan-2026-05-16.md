# Villa La Percha Launch Operating Plan - 2026-05-16

Status: internal launch plan and approval prep. No external posts, emails, DMs, paid ads, marketplace submissions, or group comments have been sent.

Primary URL: https://directstay.app/villa-la-percha

## Launch Objective

Generate qualified direct inquiries for Villa La Percha while proving which channels can produce real booking intent without relying on OTA platforms.

The first launch phase should optimize for:

1. warm-trust referrals;
2. high-intent travel-planner or concierge leads;
3. useful destination content distribution;
4. clean inquiry tracking and fast response;
5. conservative direct-booking claims backed by specific date-based comparisons when possible.

## Guardrails

- Jaimal approval is required before any external send, post, DM, comment, paid campaign, marketplace submission, or partner outreach.
- Do not imply Airbnb, VRBO, or any travel group endorses Villa La Percha.
- Do not copy OTA reviews, guest names, ratings, or photos into external outreach unless rights/permission are clear.
- Keep savings language conservative unless using a date-specific comparison from the pricing table.
- Every approved external action must be logged in `marketing/launch-outreach-ledger.md`.
- If a group or community does not allow promotional links, use location guidance without a link or skip it.

   - Highest trust.

4. **SEO/content distribution**
   - Strengthen internal links and share guide pages where relevant.
   - Focus on long-lived destination questions.

### Phase 2: Add only after tracking is clean

5. **Google Search Ads**
   - Start only after conversion tracking and inquiry delivery are proven.
   - Use high-intent terms: Chalk Sound villa rental, Taylor Bay villa, Sapodilla Bay villa, Turks and Caicos family villa.

6. **Villa marketplaces / agencies**
   - Evaluate terms before outreach.
   - This may conflict with direct-booking economics, so treat as distribution research, not default launch path.

7. **Meta/Instagram paid**
   - Useful for retargeting and credibility once traffic exists.
   - Not first priority for immediate booking intent.
